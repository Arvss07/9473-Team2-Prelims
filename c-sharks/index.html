Save our Seas – Protect Life Beneath the Breeze

Our ocean is the lifeblood of our planet, comprising the largest portion of the Earth's surface, accounting for 70%. Our seas regulates the climate, provide livelihood for millions of people worldwide, and are home to an incredible diversity of marine life.

The Beauty of Philippine Seas

In the Philippines, our ocean is deeply tied with our way of life as Filipinos. With over 7,600 islands and approximately 36,000 kilometers of coastline, the sea has shaped our Filipino culture, including but not limited to our cuisines, and it also affects our economy for generations.
