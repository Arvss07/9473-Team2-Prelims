Additionally, this website was created to be a hub of information and inspiration for ocean conservation in the Philippines and beyond. Our aim is to:

Raise awareness about the wonders of, and threats facing, our marine environment

Showcase the people, organizations, and communities working to protect the oceans

Share news and educational resources about marine science and conservation

Provide ways for individuals to get involved through lifestyle changes, volunteering, advocacy and more

Just like the starfish story, where a young girl makes a difference by throwing beached starfish back into the ocean one by one, we believe everyone can play a role, no matter how small, in saving the seas.
